A <a title="Mapping Parties" href="http://www.co-creative-recipes.cc/recipes/mapping-parties/">mapping party</a> aiming to document areas that can be accessed by disabled people.

This is usually done on Open street Map, but there are also some proprietary mobile apps that are sometimes used.

This is a kind of <a title="Couter-Mapping Party" href="http://www.co-creative-recipes.cc/recipes/couter-mapping-party/">counter mapping</a> whereby a social problem is made visible in order to induce a change.
